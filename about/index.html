About

Jun 6th, 2019 4:04 pm

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Lifeng Sun and Prof. Zhi Wang. Prior to joining [Lab of Multimedia and Networking](http://media.cs.tsinghua.edu.cn/~multimedia/], I received my B.E. degree from School of Computer Science and Technology at Xidian University in 2012. My research work focuses on the multimedia network optimization, including server selection, overlay path construction and edge-assisted content delivery. For more details, please refer to my CV [English version] [Chinese version].
